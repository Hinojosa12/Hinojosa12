Deep cleaning is recommended when: your home hasn’t been professionally cleaned in a while; you are moving into a new property; you are preparing for guests or an event; you want a seasonal home reset; your home needs a detailed sanitation. Most homeowners schedule a deep clean every 3–6 months.
