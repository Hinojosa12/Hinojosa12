Our Story

About Us – Livity Projects Inc.

At Livity Projects Inc., we are dedicated to providing reliable, high-quality cleaning and maintenance services for homes and businesses. Our goal is simple — to help our clients maintain clean, healthy, and well-kept spaces they can feel proud of.

With a team that values professionalism, efficiency, and attention to detail, we approach every project with care and commitment. Whether it's routine cleaning, deep cleaning, or property maintenance, we focus on delivering results that meet the highest standards.

We understand that every client and every space is different, which is why we work closely with our customers to provide services that fit their needs. Customer satisfaction isn't just a promise — it's the foundation of everything we do.

Our mission is to build lasting relationships with our clients by providing dependable service, consistent quality, and a team you can trust to get the job done right.
